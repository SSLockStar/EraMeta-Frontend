Our Mission: DAO the World

We are a so-called group of visionary technologists and capital allocators who are true believers in decentralized systems. Believers who also fund, build and help educate the mainstream on DAOs. We believe that all real world political, economical and social systems will benefit greatly from being decentralized. We strive to play our part in the journey towards a better world that promotes freedom and the same rules for everybody. We also get a kick from connecting and creating exclusive smart contracts between fans and their favorite creators.
