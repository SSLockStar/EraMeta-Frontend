1. Pitch your meta-business

Pitch your meta-business

    1. Application guidelines

  • To get evaluated, simply click here or the "Apply now" button below. We ask all applicants to carefully fill out the form to the best of their abilities as their applications will be reviewed by the EraMeta core team and DAO committee.

    • 2. Get evaluated for voting

  • Once you have completed your application, your submission will go through a two step evaluation processed by the EraMeta core team and DAO committee. Your business will be evaluated based on many varying factors including the problem you are solving, the solution / product you are offering, current market validation, quality of the core team and various in-house indicators.

    3. What is the next step?

  • If your business is successful during the evaluation process, you will be contacted directly by a representative of EraMeta. At this point, we have reviewed your business model and it qualifies for voting, but a formal introduction to your team is required before being presented to the DAO for resource allocation.

2. Get voted in

Get voted in
