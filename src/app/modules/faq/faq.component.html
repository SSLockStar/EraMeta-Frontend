EraMeta - The Company

What is the goal for EraMeta?

We are a group of young entrepreneurs with the aim of building a tech company that remains at the forefront of Meta-commerce innovation for decades to come. We will become the Shopify of the metaverse, integrating companies into the metaverse and providing a platform for them to manage their meta-business.

What differentiates EraMeta from its competitors?

We are a company that specializes in integrating real-life businesses into the metaverse. Our team includes strong in-house developers that are constantly innovating and designing new systems to help bridge decentralized blockchain solutions to real world problems.

What can EraMeta provide for NFT visionaries?

We can make accessible anything a project requires to go from an idea, to a finished product. This includes: Smart contract development, minting (candy machine + auctions), artwork, branding, and much more. Preferably, we work with companies looking to make the jump from web2 to web3.

Is EraMeta Hiring?

EraMeta is always looking for web3 developers and other individuals that can bring talent and value to the company. Preferably, we hire from within our community. If you think that your vision aligns with EraMeta, we recommend you contact us.

EraMeta DAO

What is EraMeta DAO?

An NFT collection composed of 3,110 entrance keys. The vision for EraMeta DAO is to build an immensely tight-knit and private community that works together to achieve success in many web3 ventures.

Will I get paid if I hold an EraMeta DAO NFT?

Yes, 50% of all launchpad profits and 10% of all metaventure profits will be gifted directly to DAO members. EraMeta also offers many valuable perks to DAO members including whitelist spots, launchpad price reduction, DAO HQ, and more!

Who & what are the Board of Directors?

The 100 gold key holders comprise the BOD. Board members will have access to weekly board meetings with the EraMeta team. They will also receive a 15% higher profit distribution than silver keys, DAO votes are worth 10, and each member will have an automatic whitelist to all future EraMeta Originals.

When is minting day?

The final minting date will be in May, BOD will be beforehand.

What is the minting price?

The minting price will be announced in the weeks leading up to the minting day.

Where will the minting take place?

Minting will take place through EraMeta. A link to the candy-machine app will be released via twitter/instagram/discord at the launch date. NFTs will be minted on the Solana network and will be listed on Solana secondary marketplaces.

EraMeta Launch Pad

What is the launchpad?

Our NFT launchpad reduces the barriers to entry in the Solana Ecosystem. We believe that the future of the NFT space is in the hands of creatives and we wan't to make it easy for anyone to start, run and grow their NFT project without having to write any code.

What are the pricing plans?

The launchpad services require a 10 ◎ (SOL) deployment fee as well as 5% of primary sales, and 5% of secondary sales. Any EraMeta DAO member will be able to launch their storefront for a reduced cost, with a 2 ◎ (SOL) deployment, 2.5% of primary sales, and 2.5% of secondary sales. 50% of launchpad profits are gifted to EraMeta DAO members.

What are EraMeta Originals?

Verified NFT collections launched with the full backing of EraMeta, from launchpad to consultation. Only quality NFTs from real companies will be released as EraMeta Originals.

How does EraMeta guarantee quality NFTs?

Our in-house team of NFT experts have developed a curated 7-factor scoring system to evaluate each partnership opportunity. Each EraMeta Original will be placed in-front of the 100 Board of Directors for further evaluation.

What are the EraMeta analyst reports?

Every EraMeta Original will be accompanied by a 1-2 page analyst report, summarizing the quality check and the rationale behind each NFT we chose to partner with. These will be attached to the public announcements for partnerships.

Who can apply for the launchpad?

Anyone can apply for access to the launchpad! However, we place a heavy focus on real established companies, looking to move into the metaverse in the future. The launchpad is free to use to anyone looking to launch a collection, only verified collections get listed on EraMeta.io.
